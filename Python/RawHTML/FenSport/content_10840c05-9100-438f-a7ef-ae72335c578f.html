Toyota GT86 2012 to 2016



Production Dates: 01/12> 12/16
Chassis Code: ZN6
Engine Code: 4U-GSE / FA20
Engine Type: Flat 4 Boxer, 16 valve
Engine Capacity: 1997cc
Maximum Engine Power: 197 BHP
Maximum Engine Torque: 205 NM
Body Type: 3-door coupe
Drive Line: Rear wheel drive
Transmission: 6-spd manual & 6 spd Auto

This section is for the earlier version Toyota GT86 from 2012 to 2016

The Toyota GT86 is a pure driver orientated sports car, featuring a 2.0L flat four boxer engine producing 197bhp, 6 speed manual or 6 speed auto (paddle shift) transmission and driving the rear wheels via a mechanical LSD. The VSC system is also switchable.

The Boxer engine uses Toyota's D-4S direct injection technology to produce 197bhp and meet modern emissions standards. The design team have also worked well to produce the car with a low centre of gravity which enables the GT 86 to corner with incredible balance and fun.

Fensport have been tuning and upgrading the GT86 since it was introduced in Summer 2012. We have a massive range of 86 tuning parts in stock and can source most products from around the world.

Please contact us if you can't find the part you are looking for, often we can help.
