Our page dedicated to the Toyota GT86 and Subaru BRZ

Here you can find information about this vehicle, as well as our best selection of products for it, if you can't find what you are looking for, don't hesitate to use the search bar or the categories via the menu at the top left.

Results of a collaboration between Toyota and Subaru, the GT86 and BRZ were launched in 2012 following a series of concepts (FT-86, BRZ Prologue...).

These 2+2 coupes share the same architecture with a 2L DOHC 4-cylinder Boxer producing 200 hp. This square engine (86 x 86 mm) features a variable intake valve timing system (Subaru AVCS) and a dual injection (Toyota D-4S). Power is transmitted to the rear wheels through a 6-speed manual or automatic transmission.

Toyota/Subaru sport coupes are known for their very low center of gravity, which, thanks to their contained weight, ensures a dynamic and fun driving experience.

As a reminder, the Toyota GT86 is sold as a Toyota 86 in Japan, but also in North America (since the withdrawal of the Scion FR-S).
